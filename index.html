How to use

  1. Select target book, chapter & verse above
  2. Tap Open Camera and allow access
  3. Hold phone over a Torah scroll column
  4. Tap Scan Now or enable Auto
  5. Follow the arrow, roll, repeat
Direction note: "Roll Right →" means unroll toward the right etz chayyim (forward in reading order). "← Roll Left" means you've passed it.

Add to Home Screen

In Safari, tap the Share button (□↑) then "Add to Home Screen" for a full-screen app with direct camera access.
